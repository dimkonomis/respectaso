How It Works

Search for a competitor by name or paste their App Store URL. The analyzer fetches their real metadata, cross-references it with keyword data, and uses LLM analysis to map the competitive landscape and build a differentiation strategy. Uses your own API key (OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google Gemini) — your data stays on your machine. Token usage is shown so you always know the cost.
