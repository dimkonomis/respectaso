How It Works

Type your proposed title, subtitle, and keyword field. Optionally select your tracked app to see real rankings. The simulator scores your metadata against your researched keywords and uses LLM analysis to suggest improvements. Uses your own API key (OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google Gemini) — your data stays on your machine. Token usage is shown so you always know the cost.
