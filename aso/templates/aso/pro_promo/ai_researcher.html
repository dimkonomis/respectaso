How It Works

Enter a seed keyword (e.g., "habit tracker") and select a country. The AI combines real App Store data with LLM analysis to produce a complete niche strategy. All processing uses your own API key (OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google Gemini) — your data stays on your machine. Token usage is shown so you always know the cost.
